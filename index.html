How variance is calculated: Bi-weekly $ budget = April 2026 monthly payroll budget × 14/30. $/Hr rate is now loaded — it sums Detailed Payroll cols G–O (wages + bonus + phone + FICA + FUTA + SUTA + state tax + work comp + admin fee) ÷ hours, averaging ~16% above base wages. Still excludes health benefits/401k/PTO accruals (those are budgeted on the ER Premiums sheet — not yet pulled in).

The KPI tile and table footer show "matched" variance only — i.e. departments that have BOTH a budget AND AOD hours this period. Budget lines for departments with zero AOD activity (typically vacant director positions, bonus/commission accruals, recruitment specialists at properties that don't have one) are surfaced separately as "unstaffed budget" in the callout above so they don't mask real overages. > 10% over flagged red, 5–10% amber, under budget green.
