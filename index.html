Source: AOD Daily Operations → Employee Exceptions report (612 enterprise employees with at least one exception), joined to Employee Details for property attribution. ELY = early-in, LTE = late-out, TDY = tardy, UNS = unscheduled punch, ABS = absent. "Creep" = ELY + LTE. AOD's exception threshold is operator-configured — confirm with your AOD admin that it matches the agreed 7-minute rule, otherwise our counts under- or over-state creep.

Why some properties show zero creep: AOD can only flag "early/late vs schedule" for employees who have schedules loaded. PSL and RVB employees do not have schedule patterns in AOD, so their punches all fall into the UNS (unscheduled) bucket and creep is undetectable. Loading schedule patterns is the prerequisite for exception monitoring at those two properties.

Coming next iteration: Long-shift (≥ 8 hr, no meal break) and missed-meal categories require punch-detail extraction (Time Card Detail per employee), not surfaced in any aggregated AOD report. Separate extraction loop needed.

Audit note (2026-04-28): The prior dashboard version showed a "Missed Punches: 3,905" tile — that figure was a column-mapping bug (the AOD "Totals" column was misread as MP). MP is not exposed in the Previous-Period view of this report; the tile has been replaced with Tardy+Absent and Unscheduled Punches.
Scope: Pay period Sun Apr 5 – Sat Apr 18, 2026 (14 days, paid Fri Apr 24, 1-week arrears). Hours and OT come straight from AOD (Reporting → Department Hours, grouped by Location Code + Department Name). Budget $ is the April 2026 monthly payroll budget from each property's Payroll sheet × 14/30 to align with the 14-day pay period.

$ variance method: Loaded $/hr rate (Detailed Payroll cols G–O ÷ hours, ~1.165× base wages) × AOD hours = estimated $ actual. Health insurance, 401k, and PTO accruals are intentionally excluded — only payroll wages, OT, ER taxes, and admin fees are in scope.

"Matched" vs "Unstaffed": KPI tile and footer show variance only for departments with both a budget AND AOD activity. Budget lines for vacant/non-existent positions (CRD, Chef, Memory Care Mgr at some properties, etc.) are surfaced separately as unstaffed budget in the callout. > 10% over flagged red, 5–10% amber, under budget green.
